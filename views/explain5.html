Move
Some parts of the code on the left side are moved to other parts of the code.
You have to "Generate Move" on both of the codes. If not, it alert warning and you cannot move or create new edit scripts.
For example, If you generate move on the left(right) side, you have to generate move on the right(left) side.

Case1.
The line "address = socketAddress.getAddress();" moves from the inside of if block to the outsidde.
